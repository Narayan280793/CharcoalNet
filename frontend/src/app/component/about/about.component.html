What We Do

Deliberately better than everyone else, CharcoalNet:

Verifies, screen, test and accurately pack Charcoal with precision in bulk or in ready-to-market packaging, auctioned to the highest bidder on our digital platform.

Produce and auction our own house brand of premium quality Briquettes and Shisha Charcoal with excellent heat retention and porosity properties.

On request, ably handle logistics to ship/deliver goods to clients worldwide. We work closely with Shipping lines to provide them with self-heating tests and weathering certificates for Charcoal.

Our products are available in FSC and Non-FSC variants. As an avid environmental and forestry management enthusiast of note, CharcoalNet is FSC certified value addition service provider.

Our stock holding level is kept at minimum 3,000 tons, securely housed, sorted and labeled for easy traceability and to ensure consistency in stock availability.

CharcoalNet have an inhouse industrial laboratory for testing the composition of Charcoal such as fixed carbon, ash content, moisture content and volatile matter.

How It Work

1

register

Registering as a Buyer is easy and FREE. Our Auctions are only available to registered users. Information provided will be verified. Click here to register.

2

Choose Product

Browse auction lots by clicking product categories: Restaurant Charcoal, Barbecue Charcoal, Charcoal Fines, Shisha Charcoal, Briquettes or Firewood.

3

Make bid

Bid on any auction by entering your bid amount above the minimum bid in the "Bid Now" box. A valid credit card or payment source is required to be on file in order to place a bid, as this is automatically processed in the event you are the winner at the close of the auction.

4

Pay Amount

If you are the successful bidder, a non-refundable 10% of the purchase price is automatically processed at the close of the auction from your payment source on file. The balance is to be paid via Wire Transfer/EFT within 72hrs. We accept PayPal, Credit Card and Wire Transfer.

5

Get Product

Receive or Collect your goods, depending on delivery option you have selected, subject to full settlement of all applicable and additional charges.
